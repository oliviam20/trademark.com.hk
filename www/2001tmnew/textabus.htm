The founders of the Company come from the legal field. The idea of providing trademark registration service through the internet is first suggested by one of our founders Miss S. W. Wong who is a lawyer and the senior partner of S.W. Wong & Associates, a Hong Kong law firm. With many years of experience in the field of trademark registration, she believes that internet will not only make the service more easily accessible to the public but will also make it more cost-effective for end users. It is against such a background that this website is set up. It is our desire to provide the same standard of service as provided by law firm, but in a more cost-effective manner.

Our mission is to provide our customers with top-level service relating or incidental to trademark registration. We strive to ensure that our services are of the highest standard. As it is our aim to provide such service through the website with a personal touch, we welcome all enquires.
